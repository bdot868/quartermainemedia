ABOUT US

In 2013, with only $500 our founders, Alberlynne “Abby” Woods and James Woods Jr., set out to become a trusted bridge for diverse social media influencers to connect with top brands and advertisers. We became THE FIRST Black-owned creative agency with full-service production capabilities to be listed in the YouTube Service Provider Directory.

Aligning a roster of cinematic Directors with social media influencers allows Quartermain Media to consistently deliver trendsetting branded content, commercials, television, film, and high performing social media campaigns for top brands to engage wide-reaching audiences on all distribution platforms.
